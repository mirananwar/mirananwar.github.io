About Me



Hello and welcome to my website! I'm a second-year at Northeastern University pursuing a combined major in Computer Science and Business Administration with a minor in Mathematics. Prior to taking my first CS class in freshman year, I had little knowledge of programming but once I took the class, I never looked back.

The first time I was exposed to technology was when my dad bought a family computer during my early years of school and I accidentally downloaded a virus on it. Despite the countless hours the 8 year old me spent in front of the computer screen trying to figure out how to remove this virus before my brown parents would yell at me, the “aha” moment was precious and so exciting that I almost downloaded another virus just so I could figure out how to remove it…

From that point on, my interest only grew as I began to learn and delve into different areas in this computing and mathematical world. The fast-pacing innovation of technology never failed to amaze me and it simply excites my innate curiosity so much more. By being part of the business and computing world, I’m able to gain knowledge and skills in order to analyze the real-world and then pursue my passion of making a positive impact on people's lives using my technological skill set. I want to be a part of this evolution of technology.

Beyond the screen, I love exploring/travelling (check out the places I’ve been to!), meeting new people or giving back to the community by volunteering at local events. I also love to read, watch movies/netflix, play badminton and I recently learned how to play the guitar! :)

Animation Player
